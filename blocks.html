Our Manifesto

Pay Once, Yours Forever

At Morse Contact, we believe in straightforward value, not endless fees. You pay once for our service, and it's yours forever—no subscriptions, no complicated plans, just simple, permanent ownership.

Endless Customization

Take full control of your widget's look and feel. From colors to its icon, you can effortlessly adjust every visual element right in your dashboard, ensuring a perfect fit for your website without needing any coding expertise.

No New App Needed

Your support team can effortlessly connect with customers using their familiar Telegram messaging app—free and available on any device. No new apps to install, just instant, convenient communication.

Your Data, Secured

At Morse Contact, safeguarding your data is paramount. We employ robust security measures to ensure your information remains private and protected, allowing you to focus on your customers.

Unmatched Accessibility & User Adoption

Telegram is a global phenomenon, boasting hundreds of millions of active users across virtually every platform imaginable. From desktop computers (Windows, macOS, Linux) to all major mobile operating systems (iOS, Android, and even web browsers), Telegram is readily available.

Absolutely Free, Forever

Cost-efficiency is paramount for businesses of all sizes, and we believe effective support shouldn't come with hidden fees. Telegram is completely free to use for both individuals and businesses. This aligns perfectly with Morse Contact's one-time payment model, ensuring that you invest in our widget once, and your communication platform remains free, forever.

Robust Security & Privacy

In an era where data privacy is paramount, Telegram stands out for its commitment to security. It offers end-to-end encryption for secret chats, and its standard cloud chats are encrypted client-to-server/server-to-client, providing a high level of protection for your customer interactions.

Superior Bot Capabilities & Developer-Friendly API

Telegram was designed with bots in mind. Its powerful and well-documented Bot API allows for sophisticated integrations, making it incredibly easy for Morse Contact to facilitate seamless communication between your website widget and your support agents.
